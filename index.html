AHS Aerospace and Aviation Club

Welcome to the American High School Aerospace and Aviation Club! We are a group of students passionate about all things aerospace and aviation. Whether you're interested in building and launching rockets, exploring the science of flight, or learning about the latest advancements in technology, our club has something for everyone. Join us as we embark on exciting projects, attend special events, and connect with others who share our love for this dynamic industry.

Events

Throughout the year, the High School Aerospace and Aviation Club hosts a variety of events designed to engage and educate our members. From rocket building competitions to drone racing challenges, our events are both fun and educational. We also organize field trips to local airports, aerospace companies, and museums to give students a behind-the-scenes look at the industry. Check our calendar regularly for updates on our upcoming events.

Projects

One of the highlights of being a member of the High School Aerospace and Aviation Club is the opportunity to work on exciting projects. From designing and launching model rockets to building and programming drones, our members are constantly challenging themselves to push the boundaries of what is possible. Our projects not only encourage creativity and teamwork but also provide valuable skills that can be applied to future careers in aerospace and aviation.

Join Us

Interested in becoming a member of the High School Aerospace and Aviation Club? Membership is open to all students who have an interest in aerospace and aviation. Whether you're a novice or an expert, there is a place for you in our club. Join us as we explore the wonders of flight and discover the possibilities of the aerospace industry. Contact us to learn more about how you can get involved.
